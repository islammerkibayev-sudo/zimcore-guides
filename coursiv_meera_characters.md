## STYLE GUIDE (apply across all scenes)

**Visual register — A24 naturalistic cinematic palette (Marriage Story / C'mon C'mon):**
- Natural daylight — warm-neutral, not pushed, not stylized. The "real room, real light" register
- Desaturated-warm tones — warm-white walls, natural wood, aged textiles, faded cottons, lived-in surfaces
- 35mm grain feel — visible naturalistic film-grain texture in skin + background (NOT digital-clean)
- NO colour grading push — no amber filter, no cool blue, no high-contrast stylization
- Wardrobe palette: muted indo-western naturals — warm cream, dusty rose, faded navy, sage green, charcoal, off-white, warm-grey linen; NO bright saturated colours

**Setting palette:**
- **Home scenes** — warm-white / cream walls with visible age, natural wood furniture, cotton textiles in faded warm tones, natural window light, brass/copper small objects, lived-in clutter
- **Tech-office scenes** — clean modern glass + concrete + warm-white, natural daylight through large windows, minimal corporate aesthetic with warm-neutral accents, subtle Indian design touches (small plants, warm-toned art suggestion)
- **Café scene** — warm-neutral independent café, natural window light, warm-wood surfaces

**Cinematographer DNA:** Celia Rowlson-Hall + Sayombhu Mukdeeprom (Call Me By Your Name natural light register) + Sean Baker observational naturalism. The "intimate-documentary-human" register.

**Anti-AI anchors (every scene):** real handheld camera micro-jitter (NOT robotic-gimbal), naturalistic facial micro-expressions (smallest eyebrow shifts, lip-tightening, real-time blink rate ~12-15/min), naturalistic body micro-shifts, naturalistic micro-pauses before/during/after speech, real-time naturalistic adult lip-sync, naturalistic adult skin texture clearly visible (visible pores, fine lines where age-appropriate, NOT smoothed). Lived-in NOT polished. Indian accent register throughout all dialogue — naturalistic modern educated Indian English cadence.

**KEY: All characters MUST read as REAL Indian adults — naturalistic South Asian skin tones, naturalistic Indian facial features, naturalistic Indian adult body proportions. NOT Western-default, NOT Bollywood-glamour, NOT stock-photo polished. The "real person you'd meet in a Mumbai or Bangalore tech company" register.**
